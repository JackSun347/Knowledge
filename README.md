# English

This is a repository dedicated to collating core knowledge points of junior high school subjects. It aims to provide concise and practical review materials for ninth-grade students, and is also suitable for students of other grades in junior high school for preview and filling knowledge gaps～

## Subjects Included

- Chemistry (knowledge points, experiments, etc.)

- History (core exam points, knowledge frameworks, etc.)

> Note: Currently, only Chemistry and History are covered. Plans for updating Morality and Rule of Law have been made, and there are no plans to update other subjects or the new version of the History textbook.
> 
> 

- Morality and Rule of Law (core exam points, knowledge frameworks, etc.) [Planned]

- Chinese (ancient poems and essays, classical Chinese content words, writing materials, etc.) [No Plan]

- Mathematics (formulas and theorems, problem-solving ideas, error-prone points, etc.) [No Plan]

- English (words and phrases, grammar exam points, writing templates, etc.) [No Plan]

- Physics (concepts and formulas, experimental principles, question type summaries, etc.) [No Plan]

- Biology (knowledge points, models) [No Plan]

- Geography (knowledge points, models) [No Plan]

## Notes

1. All documents are in Simplified Chinese, and the content is centered on junior high school textbooks and examination syllabuses;

2. All materials are for personal learning and review only; **PROHIBITED FOR COMMERCIAL USE** (including but not limited to resale, paid sharing, etc.);

3. Knowledge points will be continuously updated and improved (only for covered subjects). If you find errors or want to supplement content, please feel free to submit an issue or fork the repository and create a PR;

4. It is recommended to use it in conjunction with textbooks and exercises. Knowledge points are only sorted out, and more practice is needed for specific applications～
